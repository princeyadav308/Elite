Barbell Back Squat

Proper Form Demonstration

1 Setup

Position the barbell at mid-upper chest height on the rack. Step under the bar and rest it across your trapezius muscles.

2 The Descent

Inhale, brace your core, and initiate the movement by hinging at your hips and bending your knees simultaneously.

3 Depth

Lower until your thighs are at least parallel to the floor, ensuring your heels stay grounded and knees track over toes.

4 The Ascent

Exhale as you drive through your mid-foot to return to the starting position, keeping your chest up and spine neutral.
